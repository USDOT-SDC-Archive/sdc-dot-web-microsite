Starter Kit

The ability to scale and repeat the systems and processes associated with the USDOT Secure Data Commons (SDC) for evolving USDOT research needs is a longstanding requirement for the development team. To support the scalability and repeatability of the SDC, USDOT created a Starter Kit containing the items listed in the table below. Reviewing and using the elements in the Starter Kit accelerates the deployment of SDC and SDC-like systems to provide rapid, controlled access to research data. As the project evolves, additional items may be added to the Starter Kit.
